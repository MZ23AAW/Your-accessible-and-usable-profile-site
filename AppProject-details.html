App development

Project Description:

During my first year at the University of Hertfordshire, I created this app. The objective was to develop an application that would assist users with two essential tasks: finding accessible parking spots and managing their medications/appointments. It sought to solve practical issues that persons with disabilities frequently encounter, making sure they have the resources necessary to obtain both necessary medical care and mobility aids.

Features:

  • Medication tracking with notifications to remind users when to take their medication.
  • Location-based searches for nearby disability parking spaces.
  • User-friendly interface designed for ease of use by individuals with varying levels of tech literacy.
  • GP appointment schedule page and an emergency contact form for the user's convenience.

Role in the Project:

  • Graphical User Interface (GUI) Designer
  • Researcher

The tasks I had carried out

  • During the project's early stages, I researched fundamental aspects required for creating a user-friendly GUI to ensure effective design.
  • Used the usability document that our team leader had created as a fundamental in order to understand how the GUI should work
  • I had created a GP appointment schedule page and emergency contact form design in order to allow my team to have a visual idea on how the GUI will look like
